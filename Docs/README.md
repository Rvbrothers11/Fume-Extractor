# Fume-Extractor

My project is a 3D printed fume extractor for soldering, with a controlled fan speed and angle. It uses the traditional Arduino Nano, to control the fan speed and fan angle to save you from those tasty fumes during soldering and when you light your batteries on fire! Kidding!

The fume extractor uses 12cm X 12cm molex fan which is controlled by the mosfet motor driver and basically sucks all the air away from you while you are soldering, such that it protects your eyes and nose. 

AirBench states that solder can cause illnesses such as chronic bronchitis, chemical hypersensitivity, chest pain, headaches and dizziness which emphasizes on why we should use a fume extractor while building projects.
